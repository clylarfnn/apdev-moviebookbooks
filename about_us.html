ABOUT MovieBookBooks

MovieBookBooks was founded in 1997 in Pangasinan as a movie rental business by three friends. Since then, it has evolved into a move distribution company for Philippine cinemas. Since its transition from a movie rental business, MovieBookBooks has been the main distributor for local and foreign films for numerous cinemas in the country.

Meet The Founders

MovieBookBooks founders Clyla Rafanan, Dani Solis, and Kyla Lacson came up with the idea of as movie rental service that allowed users to order movies from a website and have the DVDs be delivered via mail.
